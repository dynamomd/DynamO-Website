Dynamo 1.3.1 Released

A bugfix release of DynamO is occurring as there were some non-trivial bugs reported and fixed recently. Please update as soon as possible to either the dynamo-1.3.1 or the master branch in the git repository.

The three bugs fixed are as follows:

  • Issue 5 (major bug): Errors in the calculation of Cv and internal energy on replica exchanged systems.
  • Issue 1 (major): Sheared systems with rectangular simulation domains fail to detect all events, leading to overlaps.
  • Issue 8 (minor): Rendered glyph sizes are incorrect in polydisperse systems.
